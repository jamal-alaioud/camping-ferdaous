☀️ Our Story

A Sanctuary
in the Olive Groves

Nestled among centuries-old olive trees on the outskirts of Marrakech, Camping Ferdaous is a rare blend of nature and comfort. Here, travelers from across the world gather — tents beside motorhomes, adventurers beside families — united by the magic of Morocco.

Our camp features stunning swimming pools, lush gardens alive with bougainvillea, resident peacocks roaming freely, and all the amenities you need for an unforgettable stay.
